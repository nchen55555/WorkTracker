Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Overview

Niche ("we", "our", "the app") is a desktop productivity application that helps you track tasks, meetings, and notes. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

What Data We Collect

When you use Niche, we may collect and store the following information:

  • Account information: Your email address and basic profile information when you sign in with Google.
  • App content: Tasks, meetings, notes, and categories you create within the app.
  • Google Calendar data: Calendar event details (title, time, attendees, location) from Google Calendars you choose to sync.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Niche app experience:

  • To authenticate your account and sync your data across sessions.
  • To display your Google Calendar events alongside your tasks and meetings.
  • To store your tasks, meetings, notes, and preferences so they persist between sessions.

How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted database platform. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Your Google Calendar data is fetched in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers beyond what is needed for display in the app.

Google Calendar Access

Niche requests read-only access to your Google Calendar data. Specifically:

  • We only read calendar events — we never create, modify, or delete events in your Google Calendar.
  • You choose which calendars to sync. You can disconnect at any time.
  • Calendar data is used only to display events within the Niche app.
  • We do not share your Google Calendar data with any third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is yours.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, all your data (tasks, meetings, notes, and account information) will be permanently removed from our systems.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at hello@theniche.tech.
